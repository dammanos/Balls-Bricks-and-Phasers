# Screenshot Shot List (Store Conversion)

## Must-have 1 — Hero Gameplay
- Moment: Ball mid-trail, combo text active (5x+), visible temple backdrop
- Goal: Show polish + speed instantly

## Must-have 2 — Powerup Impact
- Moment: Zeus lightning chaining or fireball pass-through
- Goal: Demonstrate payoff and spectacle

## Must-have 3 — Tactical Scene
- Moment: Difficult layout + paddle at edge aiming a precision rebound
- Goal: Signal skill depth

## Must-have 4 — Upgrade Choice
- Moment: Divine Blessing card selection screen
- Goal: Show progression and replayability

## Must-have 5 — Victory / Endgame
- Moment: Olympus conquered screen + score
- Goal: Completion fantasy and reward

## Capture Rules
- Keep HUD visible and readable
- Avoid empty screens (no waiting moments)
- Use native resolution (960x600)
- No browser chrome in final images
